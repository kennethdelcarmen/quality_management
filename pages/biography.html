Born on 6th of April 1922
  • Bachelors Degree in Union College, Schenectady, NY
  • Masters Degree in Massachusetts Institute of Technology’s Sloan School of Management
  • PHD in Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  • 1958 - 1968 — Director of Manufacturing Operations at General Electric
  • 1968 — Founder, president and CEO of General Systems Company of Pittsfield Massachusetts, an engineering firm that designs and install operational systems
Passed away at the age of 92 on November 13, 2014
